Lasagna recipes

World's best lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's is worth it!

It usually takes about 2-3 hours of cooking

Down below you can find the ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Now we will prepare the lasagna step by step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instruction
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving
