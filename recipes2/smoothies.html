All-Fruit Smoothies

Quick, easy smoothies made entirely with fruit

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 large banana, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries

Of course these is for a single person and you can change the serving based on the number of people.

Directions

  1. Pour the pineapple into a blender
  2. add banana
  3. strawberries
  4. blueberries
